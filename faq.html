Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the PayNakki platform and its planned services.

What is PayNakki?

PayNakki is being developed as a secure digital payment platform for individuals, merchants, and businesses.

Will PayNakki support Scan to Pay?

Yes. QR-based Scan to Pay is planned as one of the future payment features of the platform.

Can I send money using PayNakki?

Person-to-person digital money transfers are planned for future releases of the platform.

Is PayNakki secure?

Security is a core design goal. The platform is being developed with user protection and responsible security practices in mind.

Will there be a mobile app?

Yes. Android and iOS applications are planned for future development.

How can I contact PayNakki?

Please visit the Contact page for support and business enquiries.
